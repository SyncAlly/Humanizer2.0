How it works

What Humanizer extracts

Your samples are analysed locally — no text is sent to a server during this step. The profile is a set of statistics, not the samples themselves.

→
Burstiness
Variance in sentence length — the primary signal GPTZero measures. Humans mix short and long sentences; AI writes uniform ones.
→
Contraction & first-person rate
Signals formality and personal voice — AI defaults to formal and impersonal.
→
Distinctive vocabulary
Words you use regularly but aren't common stopwords — your lexical fingerprint.
→
Punctuation habits
Em dashes, ellipsis, semicolons — small signals that accumulate.
⚠
AI filler detection
If your samples contain AI filler words, they're flagged — not incorporated.
